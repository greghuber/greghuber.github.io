What's in it for you?

It's got great health benefits, including:

  • It increases cardiovascular fitness
  • It increases muscle strength and flexibility
  • It strengthens bones
  • It decreases body fat

Additionally, it has other benefits:

  • It's great for the environment
  • Cycling fundraising events help millions
  • It's a great way to build social bonds
  • It can provide time and solice for creative thinking

Here's three reasons it's great for the planet:

  1. There's zero pollution
  2. You can park 20 bicycles in the space one car takes up
  3. It takes 5% of the energy and materials to build a bike as it does a car
